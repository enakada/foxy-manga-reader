Storage Type

Let's understand the storage options!

Local: Stores your data locally. You won't be able to sync your data across devices, but you can have unlimited storage (meaning unlimited manga bookmarks).

Sync: Your data will be stored and managed by Firefox Sync account. This allows you to have your manga ready in any device running Firefox, but there is a 200kb limit to the storage (meaning that you will be limited to 280 mangas only).
